OUT AND ABOUT
Out and About aims to provide the LGBTQ+ community with information about what rights they have in new places. We also aim to provide the LGBTQ+ community with a space to talk about their experiences, recomendations, tips and warnings about places they’ve traveled to. The goal is to help LGBTQ+ people find safe spaces and understand the culture in the area. Designed hi-fi mockups and prototypes with Figma. Currently testing usability and developing with Angular.js.
